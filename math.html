Reflection
In the class I learned about the following topics: Quadratic equations and functions Polynomials & rational expressions, graphing functions, and rate of change & slope Systems of equations and inequalities & radicals Absolute value & the function concept, statistics & probability, and additional topics I found Teaching Textbooks the most useful resource in learning the material. I felt like the problems of the week were helpful in relating what I learned to real life. The most difficult part of the class was the problems of the week, as they were often quite different from what I was learning. However, they were still useful and not overly hard with the materials provided with them. I wouldn't change anything about this class.
